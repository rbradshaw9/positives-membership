# Positives Affiliate Success Guide

Use this guide to help affiliates start cleanly, share consistently, and stay payout-ready.

## The Goal

The best Positives affiliates do not "blast links." They make warm, relevant recommendations to people who are genuinely likely to benefit from a steady daily practice with Dr. Paul Jenkins.

The simplest success formula is:

1. Set up your payout details
2. Save your main affiliate link
3. Use the share kit instead of writing from scratch every time
4. Recommend Positives in real context
5. Stay consistent for a few weeks before judging results

## First Setup Checklist

Inside the affiliate portal, complete these steps first:

1. Open `My Links` and save your main affiliate link
2. Add the email connected to your PayPal account
3. Confirm your payout status shows as ready
4. Review the share kit templates
5. Copy one text version, one email version, and one newsletter/blog version so you are ready to use them

If payouts are not ready, commissions may still be tracked, but payout handling will be slower and may require manual follow-up.

## What To Share

Positives is easiest to recommend when you talk about:

- the short daily audio practice
- the calmer, steadier rhythm of the membership
- how simple it is to return to
- who it helps
- why you personally recommend it

Good framing:

- "This is a simple daily practice I actually return to."
- "If you want something grounding and usable, this is worth a look."
- "It feels more like a steady rhythm than another course to finish."

Less effective framing:

- hype-heavy language
- fake urgency
- making income claims
- implying guaranteed outcomes
- sharing with cold audiences who have no context

## Best Channels

Start with the warmest channels first:

1. Personal texts or DMs
2. Personal emails
3. Newsletter mentions
4. Webinar / live event mention
5. Social posts after you have a clear angle

Warm recommendations usually outperform broad, generic promotion.

## Suggested Weekly Rhythm

Use a simple repeatable cadence:

- `1-2` personal texts or DMs
- `1` personal email
- `1` audience mention in a newsletter, live event, or post
- `1` follow-up with someone who showed interest but did not act yet

This is enough to build traction without turning the program into a full-time job.

## How To Use The Share Kit

The affiliate portal already includes:

- a primary affiliate link
- tracked links
- quick-share templates
- email swipe copy
- downloadable creative assets

Recommended usage:

- use the text / DM copy for direct outreach
- use the email swipe for one-to-one recommendations
- use the newsletter / blog copy for audience mentions
- use tracked links when you want to understand which placements are working

Do not rewrite everything from scratch every time. Start from the provided copy and lightly personalize it.

## Payout Readiness

To stay payout-ready:

- use the PayPal email that is actually connected to your PayPal account
- keep that email current in the Positives affiliate portal
- if a payout fails, update the payout email immediately
- contact support if you are unsure which PayPal email should be used

## What Support Should Tell Affiliates

If an affiliate asks how to be successful, keep it simple:

- start with warm outreach
- share from real experience
- use the provided copy
- stay consistent for a few weeks
- make sure payout details are complete

If an affiliate asks why commissions are not being paid yet:

- confirm payout details are complete
- check for payout alerts
- confirm the referral was valid and not refunded or reversed

## What Not To Do

Affiliates should avoid:

- spam
- misleading claims
- fake scarcity
- paid traffic unless explicitly approved
- coupon-site style promotion unless explicitly approved
- promoting Positives as a get-rich-quick opportunity

The affiliate relationship should strengthen the brand, not cheapen it.

## Internal Follow-Up Ideas

Support and lifecycle automations can reinforce this guide by nudging affiliates to:

- finish payout setup
- use the share kit
- copy their referral link
- make their first recommendation
- return after their first tracked click or first commission

## Success Markers

The clearest early signs of healthy affiliate momentum are:

- payout details completed
- tracked links created
- first clicks recorded
- first qualified referral
- first approved commission

These matter more than whether someone posts immediately on every channel.

## Short Version

If we want the shortest coaching version for an affiliate, it is this:

1. Get payout-ready
2. Share with warm people first
3. Use the templates
4. Recommend Positives naturally
5. Repeat weekly
