# Beta Closeout Survey Template

## Purpose

Use this near the end of the beta phase to learn what actually mattered to testers, what still feels weak, and who may become strong long-term members or advocates.

## Suggested Format

Keep it short. Five to seven questions is enough.

## Questions

1. What part of Positives felt most valuable to you?

2. What felt most confusing, unfinished, or frustrating?

3. Did you hit any issue that made you stop, hesitate, or avoid using part of the product?

4. What would you improve first if you were us?

5. Which parts did you actually use?
   - Today
   - My Practice
   - Library
   - Courses
   - Events
   - Coaching
   - Billing / Account

6. How likely would you be to continue after beta?
   - very likely
   - maybe
   - unlikely

7. Would you be open to:
   - a short testimonial later
   - a follow-up conversation
   - continuing at a founding/beta rate

## Tone Guidance

- appreciative
- concise
- curious
- not defensive

## Internal Use

Review responses for:

- strong retention signals
- repeated friction patterns
- feature priorities
- possible testimonials
- founding-member candidates
