# VSL Sales Page Spec

## Purpose

Define the Dr. Paul-led video sales page.

This page is not the control.

It is the trust-heavy alternative for people who need more:

- story
- credibility
- emotional buy-in
- time with Dr. Paul before purchasing

## Canonical Route

- proposed route: `/watch`

## Offer

- same paid offer as the control page
- no free trial on this page in V1
- same pricing truth as `/join`
- same 30-day guarantee

## Primary Audience

- warm traffic
- podcast traffic
- email traffic
- retargeting
- audiences coming from Dr. Paul appearances

## Page Job

Help a visitor feel:

- I trust Dr. Paul
- this is for me
- this is different from generic self-help content
- I’m ready to pay now

## Core Structure

### 1. Minimal Header

- logo
- optional `Sign in`
- avoid a distracting nav

### 2. Hero + VSL Block

- strong promise headline
- video from Dr. Paul
- short support copy under the player
- CTA directly below the player

### 3. What Positives Actually Is

- daily practice
- weekly reflection
- monthly theme
- not a course

### 4. Why People Need This

- reacting vs responding
- emotional overwhelm
- wanting steadiness, clarity, peace

### 5. Proof / Trust

- Dr. Paul credibility
- testimonial-ready area
- calm trust signals

### 6. Pricing + Offer

- direct-to-paid
- clear level choice
- 30-day guarantee

### 7. FAQ

- shorter than the control-page FAQ
- only the questions that block purchase

### 8. Final CTA

- direct paid CTA

## Video Rules

- lead with Dr. Paul as the guide
- keep surrounding copy light
- page should still work if the final video asset is not ready yet
- implementation can launch with:
  - embedded placeholder video block
  - or a polished poster/coming-soon video shell

## CTA Rules

Primary CTA:

- `Join Positives`
- secondary supporting copy:
  - `Start with the level that fits you`

The VSL page should send users into the same paid conversion path as the control page.

## Design Rules

- stronger emotional center than `/join`
- fewer competing sections
- more vertical rhythm
- video must feel premium on mobile and desktop

## Analytics Requirements

- pageview tracking
- CTA click tracking to `/join` or checkout
- preserve affiliate attribution when a VSL page is used from an affiliate source

## Success Criteria

- the page increases trust for warmer audiences
- it does not change the underlying paid offer
- it remains easy to compare against the control page

## Build Notes

- build should support a configurable video/embed block
- add a follow-up content swap step for the final Dr. Paul VSL asset if needed
